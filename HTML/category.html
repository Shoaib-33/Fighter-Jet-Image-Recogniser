Categories of Military Aircraft

  • Fighter Jets: High-performance aircraft designed for air-to-air combat
  • Interceptors: Aircraft designed for intercepting enemy aircraft
  • Bombers: Aircraft designed for strategic or tactical bombing missions
  • Attack Aircraft: Aircraft specialized for ground attack missions
  • Reconnaissance Aircraft: Aircraft used for intelligence gathering and surveillance
  • Multirole Aircraft: Versatile aircraft designed to perform multiple roles
